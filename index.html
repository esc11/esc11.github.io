Who am I?

I'm Elisabeth, an upcoming Senior studying Informatics, concentrating in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Washington.
UX designer with an insatiable curiosity and an immense passion for designing meaningful interactions between people and technology that solve complex problems. Global nomad, aggressive typist, and future crazy cat lady.
