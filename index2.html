I believe that technology is cultural.

We're the products of where we come from;
the products and experiences we design inherit our values, our assumptions, our perspective.

For me, "where are you from?" is a pretty difficult question.

I grew up moving often,
at an average of every two years.

So I don't feel that I belong to a single culture.

I think it's made me more aware of our differences,
and our similarities too...

...how to understand them, reconcile them, celebrate them...

...and eager to design for them.
