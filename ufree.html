  Design a solution to "surviving college" with a team of three

My team chose to highlight the problem of college students' loneliness and sense of isolation. Through our research and interviews with students, we found that the root of the problem was associated with the students' lack of time to be intentional about their relationships. To provide a solution for maintaining and strengthening friendships, we designed a mobile application that displays contacts' availability statuses in real-time, following a designated weekly schedule.

I was responsible for designing the prototypes on Axure and writing the application details of the design specification. My process book further outlines our iterations, my contribution, and how we made our design choices.
